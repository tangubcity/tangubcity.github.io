The Tangub City National High School was established by virtue of City Resolution No. 304 of the Sanggunian Panglungsod in June 1971. It is located at Mantic, Tangub City in the Provice of Misamis Occidental, Region X - Northern Mindanao, bearing a School ID 304171.

The main objective for its creation was to meet the needs of poor but deserving students to obtain quality general secondary education who don't have enough financial means to study in a private school.

A total of 68 classrooms, 14 offices and ongoing classroom construction in its 43,397 suare meter land area. The accelerated development of the school and its facilities can be attributed to the efforts of the local and national officials.
